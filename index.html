    To search UFO sighting data, please use the search dropdown that can be activated by clicking the SEARCH button in the navbar. From the search dropdown you can select the search criteria you like to use to filter your search by checking the checkbox next to any search term you would like to include in your search. To initiate your search, click the SEARCH button in the dropdown. If you would like to clear your search results and all of the checkboxes you have previously checked, click the CLEAR FORM button in the dropdown. You can view our entire database by leaving all of the checkboxes blank and clicking SEARCH.

    Thank you for using the UFO Secrets database search utility.
